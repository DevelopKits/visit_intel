Testing OSPRay with example datasets
Details from http://ospray.github.io/demos.html

Note: Ingo has stated the GLUT-based viewers (ospModelViewer, etc.) may be
      deprecated soon.

Download all examples:
  - Copy osp_examples.tar and expand it in examples/
    - Contains the 8 examples from the demos website in structured folders 

Environment variables:
  - 

Running:
  - The following are for Xeon. To run on Phi:
    - Append --osp:coi to all commands
    - export OSPRAY_COI_WORKER=<ospray_build_dir>/ospray_coi_worker.mic
    - export SINK_LD_LIBRARY_PATH=<ospray_build_dir>

  - magnetic-1024-iso
    - ./ospModelViewer ../examples/m1024/magnetic-1024-iso.x3d --renderer ao4

  - magnetic-512-volume
    - ./ospVolumeViewer ../examples/m512/magnetic-512-volume.osp

  - llnl-2048-iso
    - ./ospModelViewer ../examples/llnl/llnl-2048-iso.xml --renderer ao4

  - csafe-heptane-302-volume
    - ./ospVolumeViewer ../examples/csafe/csafe-heptane-302-volume.osp

  - tacc-turbulence-256-volume
    - ./ospVolumeViewer ../examples/tt256/tacc-turbulence-256-volume.osp

  - fiu-groundwater
    - ./ospModelViewer ../examples/fiu/fiu-groundwater.xml --renderer ao
    - ./ospModelViewer ../examples/fiu/fiu-groundwater.xml --renderer obj --sun-dir -1.5 -1 -1

  - xfrog-forest
    - ./ospModelViewer ../examples/xfrog/xfrog-forest.xml --renderer obj

  - NASA-B-field-sun
    - ./ospStreamLineViewer ../examples/nasa/NASA-B-field-sun.osx --renderer ao4

Note: XFrog forest is known to cause a long delay before rendering. This is due
      to the high number of triangles in the model.

Known controls:
  - WASD, camera tilt/rotate
  - F, fullscreen
  - R, reset view
  - Left mouse click & drag, tilt/rotate
  - Right mouse click & drag, zoom
  - Middle mouse click & drag, pan
